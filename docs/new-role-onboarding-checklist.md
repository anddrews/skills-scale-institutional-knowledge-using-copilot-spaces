# New Role Onboarding Checklist

## Purpose
Ensure smooth onboarding for team members joining a project in any role, with role-specific guidance to accelerate contribution and integration.

## When to use
Use this checklist whenever a new team member joins the project or transitions into a new role on the team.

---

## General Onboarding (All Roles)

### Week 1: Orientation & Access
- [ ] Receive welcome email with project overview and team roster
- [ ] Get access to project repository and documentation
- [ ] Join project communication channels (Slack, Teams, etc.)
- [ ] Review [Project Management Overview](octoacme-project-management-overview.md)
- [ ] Review [Roles and Personas](octoacme-roles-and-personas.md) to understand team structure
- [ ] Attend project kickoff or overview meeting
- [ ] Meet 1:1 with Project Manager to discuss role and expectations
- [ ] Review project charter and current status

### Week 2: Deep Dive
- [ ] Review project roadmap and current sprint/iteration plan
- [ ] Understand project success metrics and key milestones
- [ ] Review risk register and known dependencies
- [ ] Shadow key meetings relevant to your role
- [ ] Begin contributing to discussions and reviews

### Ongoing
- [ ] Establish regular sync cadence with key collaborators
- [ ] Set up recurring meetings as appropriate for your role
- [ ] Identify and document any questions or improvement opportunities

---

## Role-Specific Onboarding

### For Developers
- [ ] Set up local development environment
- [ ] Review codebase structure and architecture documentation
- [ ] Complete first small task or bug fix to learn the workflow
- [ ] Understand testing strategy and CI/CD pipeline
- [ ] Review coding standards and PR review process
- [ ] Join daily standups and sprint planning meetings

### For Product Managers
- [ ] Review product vision and strategy documents
- [ ] Access product analytics and metrics dashboards
- [ ] Meet with key stakeholders to understand priorities
- [ ] Review customer feedback and research findings
- [ ] Understand backlog prioritization criteria
- [ ] Review acceptance criteria templates and definition of done

### For Project Managers
- [ ] Review project plan, timeline, and dependencies
- [ ] Access project tracking tools (Jira, Azure DevOps, etc.)
- [ ] Set up status reporting templates and cadence
- [ ] Review communication plan and stakeholder list
- [ ] Understand escalation paths and decision-making process
- [ ] Schedule regular check-ins with Product Manager and team leads

### For Portfolio Managers
- [ ] Review organizational strategic objectives and priorities
- [ ] Access portfolio management tools and dashboards
- [ ] Meet with Executive Sponsors and functional leads
- [ ] Review resource allocation across current projects
- [ ] Understand portfolio governance and reporting requirements
- [ ] Review historical portfolio performance metrics

### For Change Managers
- [ ] Review change management policy and procedures
- [ ] Access change tracking and approval systems
- [ ] Understand change advisory board structure and schedule
- [ ] Review recent changes and lessons learned
- [ ] Meet with Risk Manager and Operations teams
- [ ] Understand training and communication channels available

### For Quality Assurance Leads
- [ ] Review quality standards and testing strategy
- [ ] Access test management and defect tracking tools
- [ ] Review test environments and data requirements
- [ ] Understand release criteria and quality gates
- [ ] Meet with development leads to discuss quality practices
- [ ] Review recent quality metrics and trends

### For Stakeholder Liaisons
- [ ] Review complete stakeholder list and engagement plan
- [ ] Understand stakeholder priorities and concerns
- [ ] Review communication templates and channels
- [ ] Schedule introductory meetings with key stakeholders
- [ ] Access feedback collection tools (surveys, forms)
- [ ] Understand escalation process for stakeholder issues

## 30-60-90 Day Goals Template

### 30 Days
- Understand the project context, goals, and team dynamics
- Establish working relationships with key collaborators
- Begin contributing in your role (attend meetings, provide input)
- Identify 1-2 quick wins or improvements you can contribute

### 60 Days
- Take ownership of key responsibilities in your role
- Lead or co-lead role-specific activities
- Proactively identify and address gaps or risks
- Build trust and credibility with the team

### 90 Days
- Operate independently in your role with minimal oversight
- Contribute to strategic decisions and planning
- Mentor or support others in similar roles
- Recommend process improvements based on your experience

## Checklist for Onboarding Facilitator

**Project Manager or Team Lead:**
- [ ] Send welcome email with project overview
- [ ] Ensure all access and tools are provisioned
- [ ] Schedule 1:1 kickoff meeting
- [ ] Assign an onboarding buddy or mentor
- [ ] Share role-specific documentation and resources
- [ ] Check in at 1 week, 2 weeks, and 30 days
- [ ] Collect onboarding feedback at 30-day mark
- [ ] Update onboarding checklist based on feedback
